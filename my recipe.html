Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Chili powder
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Bell Pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Curd
  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cucumbers

Preparing Chicken Sandwich

  1. Take Chicken meat in a bowl
  2. Add the spice powders(Salt,chili powders)
  3. Add 2 tablespoon fresh curd (yogurt). Mix very well.
  4. Marinate the chicken for 45 mins
  5. Fry the marinated chicken on both sides on a pan
  6. Take the bread slices and spread some butter on them and toast them
  7. Spread cucumbers and carrots on the slices.
  8. Now place some Lettuce,thin onion slices and sliced bell pepper.Add the chicken stuffing and your chicken sandwhich is ready!
